---
title: "Change Your Email or Password"
---

1. Go to [rive.app/account](https://rive.app/account)
2. Make sure you're on the **Account** tab

**To change your email:**

- Click "Edit Profile" on the right
- Update your email
- Click "Save changes"

**To change your password:**

- Click "Change Password"
- Enter your new password
- Click "Save Password"

**Questions?** [support@rive.app](mailto:support@rive.app)
