## Our Policy

## Billing Errors

If you were charged incorrectly (duplicate charge, charged after cancellation, wrong amount), email [support@rive.app](mailto:support@rive.app) with:

- Your account email
- Date and amount of the charge
- What went wrong

We'll investigate and fix any legitimate billing errors.

## Before You Upgrade

If you're not sure Rive is right for you:

- Use the free plan first to test it out
- Start with monthly instead of annual if you want flexibility
- Double-check you're upgrading the right workspace
