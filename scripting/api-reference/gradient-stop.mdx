---
title: GradientStop
---

A color stop in a gradient, defined by its position in the range [0, 1] and
the color at that position.


## Fields

### `position`

A color stop in a gradient, defined by its position in the range [0, 1] and
the color at that position.
Position of the stop along the gradient, where 0 is the start and 1 is
the end.


### `color`

Color at the specified position.
