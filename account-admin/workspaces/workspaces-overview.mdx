title: "Workspaces Overview"
---

Workspaces are a way for individuals, teams, and companies to organize private and collaborative files. You can work solo or invite unlimited users to a Workspace. Everyone has their own Workspace, and all teams are given a dedicated Workspace.

Rive allows you to have a mix of paid and free Workspaces. Upgrading a Workspace to paid does **not** upgrade all of your workspaces.

The switcher makes it easy to toggle between different Workspaces. For example, you can switch between your personal Workspace, your company Workspace, or a Workspace youâ€™ve been temporarily invited to as a freelancer.
