## Member Roles

| Role       | What they can do               | Cost      |
| :--------- | :----------------------------- | :-------- |
| **Viewer** | View files only                | Free      |
| **Editor** | View and edit files            | Paid seat |
| **Admin**  | View, edit, and manage members | Paid seat |

## Inviting Users to a Workspace

Inviting a user to a workspace gives them access to all projects within that workspace.

<Info>
  If you only want to give a member access to a specific project, open that project and invite them from the right sidebar.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to [Accounts](https://rive.app/account/teams/).
  </Step>
   <Step>
    Click **Manage Workspace** next to the workspace you'd like add a member to.
  </Step>
   <Step>
    Under **Members**, click **Invite Members**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Enter the member's email, select their role, and click **Confirm**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
For paid workspaces, you're charged a pro-rated amount immediately for any Editor or Admin seats.
</Note>

<Info>
  **Troubleshooting**

  If you can't invite a member, make sure your email address is verified. Go to [rive.app/account](https://rive.app/account?utm_source=docs&utm_medium=content), open the **Profile** tab, and click **Resend verification email** if needed.
</Info>


## Accepting an Invite to a Workspace

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to [Accounts](https://rive.app/account/) and makes sure you're logged into the correct Rive account.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Follow the invite link in your email.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  **Troubleshooting**

  Don't see the email? Check the spam folder.
</Info>
