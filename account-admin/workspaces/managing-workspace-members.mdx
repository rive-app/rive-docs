## Member Roles

| Role       | What they can do               | Cost      |
| :--------- | :----------------------------- | :-------- |
| **Viewer** | View files only                | Free      |
| **Editor** | View and edit files            | Paid seat |
| **Admin**  | View, edit, and manage members | Paid seat |

## Inviting Members to a Workspace

Inviting a member to a workspace gives them access to all projects within that workspace.

<Info>
  If you only want to give a member access to a specific project, open that project and invite them from the right sidebar.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to [Accounts](https://rive.app/account/teams/).
  </Step>
   <Step>
    Click **Manage Workspace** next to the workspace you'd like add a member to.
  </Step>
   <Step>
    Under **Members**, click **Invite Members**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Enter the member's email, select their role, and click **Confirm**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
For paid workspaces, you're charged a pro-rated amount immediately for any Editor or Admin seats.
</Note>

## Changing Member Roles

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to [Accounts](https://rive.app/account/teams/).
  </Step>
   <Step>
    Click **Manage Workspace** next to the workspace you'd like add a update the roles for.
  </Step>
   <Step>
    Under **Members**, click **Manage Roles**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Select the role and click **Confirm**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
For paid workspaces, you're charged a pro-rated amount immediately for any Editor or Admin seats.
</Note>

## Removing Members from a Workspace

Admins can remove members from a workspace at any time, including yourself.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to [Accounts](https://rive.app/account/teams/).
  </Step>
   <Step>
    Click **Manage Workspace** next to the workspace you'd like add a update the roles for.
  </Step>
   <Step>
    Under **Members**, click the `x` next to the member you'd like to remove.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
The workspace owner cannot be removed. If you need to change ownership, [contact support](https://airtable.com/appUtquW7bOB1IIqA/pagzVBE4tO5QdjWTM/form).
</Note>

## FAQs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I can't invite or remove a member.">
     If you can't invite or remove a member, make sure your email address is verified. Go to [rive.app/account](https://rive.app/account?utm_source=docs&utm_medium=content), open the **Profile** tab, and click **Resend verification email** if needed.
  </Accordion>
	<Accordion title="Why do we both have to pay to collaborate? We both have paid workspaces.">
    Rive charges per seat per workspace. If you're an editor in multiple workspaces, each workspace pays for your seat separately. To collaborate, one of you invites the other to their workspace—and that workspace pays for the seat.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I can't see the invite email.">
    Check your spam folder and double check that the invite email is correct.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
